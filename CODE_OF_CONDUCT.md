# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

**The Heildamm Components community is dedicated to providing an inclusive, respectful, and harassment-free environment for all participants.** We are committed to fostering a welcoming community that encourages collaboration, innovation, and mutual respect regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

We pledge to make participation in this project and our community a positive and constructive experience for everyone.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors We Encourage

- **Respectful Communication**: Use professional, courteous language in all interactions
- **Inclusive Language**: Be mindful of and respect diverse backgrounds, perspectives, and identities
- **Active Listening**: Listen genuinely to others' viewpoints and seek to understand
- **Constructive Feedback**: Provide feedback that is specific, actionable, and focused on improvement
- **Empathy and Compassion**: Approach conflicts with understanding and good faith
- **Blame-Free Problem Solving**: Focus on solutions rather than assigning fault
- **Collaboration**: Work together toward common goals with mutual respect
- **Appreciation of Diversity**: Value and appreciate the unique contributions of all participants

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated in this community:

- **Harassment**: Any form of harassment, discrimination, or unwelcome attention based on personal characteristics
- **Prejudice and Discrimination**: Exclusion or discrimination based on:
  - Race, color, or ethnicity
  - Gender, gender identity, or gender expression
  - Sexual orientation or romantic orientation
  - Age, disability, or illness
  - National origin, socioeconomic status, or religion
  - Political beliefs or affiliations
- **Intimidation and Threats**: Intimidating, threatening, or aggressive communication
- **Trolling and Disruptive Behavior**: Deliberately disruptive comments, posts, or actions
- **Personal Attacks**: Attacks on individuals rather than their ideas or arguments
- **Unwelcome Conduct**: Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or other sexual conduct
- **Doxxing**: Publishing private personal information without consent
- **Hate Speech**: Content promoting violence or hatred toward individuals or groups
- **Abusive Language**: Vulgar, abusive, or dehumanizing language directed at individuals or groups

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces, including repository issues, pull requests, and discussions
- Email communications with maintainers and community members
- Public communications related to the project on social media platforms
- Official project events and conferences
- Unofficial community gatherings organized in the project's context
- Any situation where a person is representing the Heildamm Components project

---

## Enforcement

###Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates this Code of Conduct:

1. **Document the Incident**
   - Record substantial details about what occurred
   - Note dates, times, and any witnesses
   - Preserve evidence if possible (screenshots, links, etc.)

2. **Report to Maintainers**
   - Contact project maintainers through appropriate channels
   - Use private communication methods for sensitive reports
   - Provide your report to multiple maintainers if necessary

3. **What to Include in Your Report**
   - A clear and specific description of the violation
   - Identification of involved parties (if comfortable sharing)
   - Context and relevant background information
   - Impact of the behavior
   - Preferred outcome or desired resolution

### Confidentiality and Privacy

- Reports will be handled confidentially
- Information will be shared only with those directly involved in investigation and response
- Reporter identity will be protected when possible
- Retaliatory actions against reporters are strictly prohibited

### Investigation Process

1. **Initial Review**: Maintainers acknowledge receipt and provide timeline for investigation
2. **Investigation**: Thorough, impartial investigation of the reported behavior
3. **Response**: Appropriate action taken based on investigation findings
4. **Resolution**: Both parties informed of outcome and any corrective action

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

- **Verbal Warning**: Private discussion about the violation
- **Written Warning**: Documented communication about unacceptable behavior
- **Temporary Suspension**: Temporary restriction from contributing or participating
- **Permanent Removal**: Removal from the project and community spaces

The severity of consequences will match the severity and pattern of the violation.

---

## Appeals Process

1. **Right to Appeal**: Individuals can appeal enforcement decisions within 30 days
2. **Appeal Submission**: Submit appeals with additional context or new information
3. **Appeal Review**: Independent review by different maintainers
4. **Final Decision**: Appeal decision is final

---

## Community Responsibilities

### All Community Members

- Hold yourself and others to this Code of Conduct
- Address violations respectfully and directly when safe to do so
- Support creating a safe, inclusive community environment
- Assume good faith while watching for harmful patterns

### Maintainers and Leaders

- Model exemplary behavior and conduct
- Enforce this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Create a welcoming environment for all participants
- Provide education and resources on respectful collaboration
